About DK TEX

A calm, reliable production partner for European companies.

DK TEX is a Latvian sewing production company focused on long-term cooperation, careful production and clear communication.

The company continues the production traditions and accumulated experience connected with SIA FIRMA JATA, which worked with European partners for many years.

Our role is to support companies looking for a stable sewing production partner in the Baltic region and across the European market.
